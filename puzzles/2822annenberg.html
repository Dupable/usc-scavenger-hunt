There's something really freeing to me about high places. And I feel an inkling of it when I sit up here at night.

"Sonder...I used to be obsessed with it. But...what about the idea of un-sonder? I don't want to stare in awe at these lives I'll never fully know. No. That's not enough. I *want* to know...
